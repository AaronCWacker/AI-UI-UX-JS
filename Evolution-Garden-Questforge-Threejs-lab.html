EVOLUTION GARDEN

A single-file Three.js crossover prototype for quickly testing what we actually enjoy: ship navigation, joust-style action, boardgame cards, merchant orders, memory tiles, and visible evolution.

🛶 WORLD BODY

Drive a longship through a procedural fjord. Water, islands, ruins, settlements, monsters, and resource nodes create the physical board.

🃏 RULE CARDS

Each design idea is a card. Fire cards with keys, watch cooldowns, and judge whether the mechanic earns its space.

🧬 VISIBLE EVOLUTION

Identity, inventory, risk, story, tactics, and memory meters rise based on what you do. This makes taste-testing measurable.
