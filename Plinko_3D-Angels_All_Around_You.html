A Prayer for My Love, Sam

Dear Lord, help me this day to be...

1. Physical: to walk with you, hand in hand, in pleasure and intimacy as we adventure.

2. Emotion: to support you, make you smile and laugh, and be your biggest fan.

3. Spirit: To lift your heart, help you dream, and help you be your best.

4. Pure: To be selfless, being the best version of myself to be worthy of your love.

5. Family: To be dependable, smart, safe, and responsible so we can grow together.

6. Love: To satisfy you, make your eyes sparkle, to wipe your tears in happiness of love.

7. Respect: To be your best friend, honoring your wants, needs and dreams.

8. Trust: To be an open book to you, one you can always depend on to be there when you need me.

9. Commit: To be hopelessly devoted favorite sunrise to sunset.

10. Bond: To communicate with you even without words.
