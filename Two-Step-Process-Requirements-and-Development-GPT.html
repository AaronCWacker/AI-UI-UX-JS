🚀 Fastest Beautiful Path

  1. Make or print a small master.
  2. Sand, prime, and seal it perfectly.
  3. Use a simple silicone block or two-part mold.
  4. Cast resin thin, then backfill or foam-fill.
  5. Chrome/candy-clear for the pop-art mirror look.

🧯 Studio Safety

Use ventilation, nitrile gloves, eye protection, covered surfaces, and the resin maker’s SDS/technical sheet. Mix tiny test batches before committing to a finished piece.
