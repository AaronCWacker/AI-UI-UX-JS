⛏️ PURCHASING AGENT BRIEF

  • GO: WI repealed its sulfide-mining moratorium in 2017 — the Bend (Cu·Au) and Reef (Au) prospects became legally viable overnight. Taylor & Marathon Co land is still priced as farmland.
  • GO: MN's Duluth Complex (Ely–Babbitt–Hoyt Lakes arc) holds one of Earth's largest undeveloped Cu·Ni·PGE endowments. Tamarack is the highest-grade nickel play in the US — Aitkin Co acreage remains quiet.
  • CAUTION: Anything inside the BWCA watershed (Twin Metals) carries extreme federal permitting risk — leases revoked once already. NorthMet spent a decade in litigation. Buy the timeline, not the headline.
  • NEVER: Pipestone MN — catlinite quarrying is reserved to Native Americans by federal law. Crandon WI — the deposit was purchased by the Sokaogon Chippewa & Potawatomi in 2003 explicitly to end mining. Both are permanently off the board.
  • GO: Frac sand: buy along rail in Trempealeau / Chippewa Co WI. Cuyuna (Crosby MN) manganese is being restudied for battery-grade Mn.
  • NICHE: Agate (Moose Lake) & thomsonite (Grand Marais) = small parcels, lapidary + tourism revenue, not industrial scale.
