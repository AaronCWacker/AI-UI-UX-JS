🧮 Capacity Assumptions

Important: this is a what-if simulator, not live hospital capacity. Real wait time depends on staffed beds, staffing mix, ED queue, case acuity, service line, local transfer status, and specialty availability.

Formula: theoretical turns/day = staffed beds × occupancy ÷ avg stay. The doctor-pressure proxy is a comparison index only; it is not a physician staffing measure.
Data lineage: TX and FL use Definitive Healthcare staffed-bed tables; NM uses Definitive Healthcare’s top-NPR table with staffed beds then sorts it for this demo; MN is seeded from your uploaded MN tour app and public references. ASC counts use Definitive Healthcare’s 2025 surgery-center-by-state table. Coordinates are approximate demo pins.

📊 Fact-check Tables
